About Lord Panduranga

Lord Panduranga, also known as Vithoba or Vitthala, is a revered Hindu deity predominantly worshipped in the Indian states of Maharashtra, Karnataka, Goa, Telangana, and Andhra Pradesh. He is generally considered a manifestation of Lord Vishnu or his avatar, Krishna.

Vithoba is often depicted as a dark young boy, standing arms-akimbo on a brick, sometimes accompanied by his main consort Rakhumai (Rukmini). The main center of Vithoba's worship is Pandharpur in Maharashtra, which is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India.
